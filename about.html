My Story

I first learned the basics of HTML and CSS as a Sophomore in High School. That was the first time I ever programmed anything outside ofscracth. Only starting in 2020, I took an AP Computer Science Principals class as a Junior, and fell in love with JavaScript. My dad had always been a computerperson and we always did projects with computers whenever we got time to. I knew at some point between middle school and high school that I wanted to followin his footsteps and go into computer science. Since then I have been coding as much as possible and doing as much research as possible, taking all of the opportunities that I can get, and that comes to where I am now

Where I am now

As of February 2022, I am a 16 year old JavaScript/HTML/CSS tutor for a high school of 3000 active students, getting into some other languages like Python and Java,experimenting with frameworks like selenium and React.js. I am always building my portfolio, and always looking for new opportunities to improve my skills.

My goals
